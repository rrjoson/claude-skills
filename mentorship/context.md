## Application Rubric

### Disqualifying Red Flags (any one = recommend Decline)
- Wrong name or generic greeting (e.g. "Hi Alex" or "Hi there" — my name is Ricardo)
- Clearly a copy-paste blast sent to many mentors
- Goal is to land a first dev job / break into tech

### Soft Red Flags (lower score)
- No specific goal — just "want to grow" or "need guidance"
- No self-awareness about what's blocking them
- Vague ask: "help me with JavaScript" with no context

### Green Flags (raise score)
- Uses correct name, references my specific background
- Concrete goal with timeframe (e.g. "get promoted to senior in 6 months")
- Shows self-awareness about their gaps
- Already working as an engineer wanting to level up
- Specific challenge they want help with
- Goal aligns with my focus areas above
